Lampi

Python, C++, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Swift

Lampi is a desklamp connected to a real time Web Service and iOS app (via MQTT and Bluetooth, respectively) paired with on-board touch screen controls for RGB LEDs connected to a RaspberryPi serving as a stepping stone into the IoT world. "Smart" RC

Python

As a final project for a highschool course, I worked with a partner to modify an old RC car to navigate away from obstacles on a path. It was an old car, so we had to make modifications to the old wiring for powering the car and added ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles within a few feet of the car.

Ocubano

C++

Ocubano was a final project for a Case Western Reserve University SAGES course (USNA288R Fall '18). Our goal was to work in a small group to create an IoT device (using a Particle Photon) and market it to the class as a product they could use. The Ocubano served as a notifier of whether a bathroom was vacant or not by using an ultrasonic sensor to monitor a bathroom doorway and publish to an LED matrix.
